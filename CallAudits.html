Case Study: Call Center QA App

This project is based on work I performed as a Data Analyst working at a high volume call center where there was a drastic need to overhaul the Quality Assurance process. Though the implementation differs slightly, the intent of this project is to showcase core skills used to overcome problems faced by the organization utilizing familiar technologies. The following is a summary with code snippits from this repo (WIP).

The Problem

At the time, the company was tech-immature and new to even having a dedicated analytics team, spreadsheets and emails served as the core tools underlying most business functions. The call center staffed somewhere around 600 dialers across 30 teams, with only 2-3 Quality Assurance agents responsible for auditing and providing feedback for call volumes in the range of millions per day. A standardized form existed with pass/fail categories, where QA would listen to a selection of calls submitted by team leaders and respond via email their findings and follow up with coaching.

The core issues I identified in the process are:

  • Lack of historical data tracking
  • Indefinite/nonstandard procedure for selecting calls
  • Low coverage due to small sample
In the process of redefining the auditing procedure, I sought to answer these questions for the business:
  • Who are our highest and lowest performers?
  • What is the impact of quality on our KPI's?
  • Where are our greatest areas of opportunity?
  • How is call quality changing over time?
